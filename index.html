The "Shiny" Lie

Walk into a supermarket, and you'll see imported fruits that look perfect. Shiny, spotless, staying hard for weeks.

But have you ever asked why?

That shine isn't natureit's wax. To survive months in shipping containers, imported fruits are dipped in chemical preservatives. They're "mummified" fruit.
